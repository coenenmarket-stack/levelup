Privacy Policy

Level Up Life · Last updated: July 22, 2026

Level Up Life (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a gamified personal-growth app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Level Up Life on the web or iOS.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, and authentication details when you sign up with email/password or Google.
  • Profile & gameplay data: quests, XP, levels, achievements, coach preferences, and settings you create in the app.
  • User content: optional avatar photos you choose to upload.
  • Usage & diagnostics: basic app usage, crash, and performance data to keep the service reliable.
  • Device information: device type, OS version, and app version.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and improve Level Up Life (quests, progress, AI coaching features)
  • Authenticate your account and sync your data across devices
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI features

Some coaching and quest suggestions use AI processing. Content you submit for coaching may be sent to our AI providers solely to generate responses for you. We do not sell your personal data.

4. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers that host the app, authentication, storage, and AI features (for example Firebase / Google Cloud and AI model providers), under appropriate agreements
  • Legal requirements when required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business transfers if we are involved in a merger or acquisition, with notice where required

5. Data retention & deletion

We retain account and gameplay data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us at the email below. We may retain limited records as required by law or for security.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. Contact us to make a request.

7. Children’s privacy

Level Up Life is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use industry-standard protections, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
